Nexa Bot Privacy Policy

Nexa Bot respects your privacy. This page explains what data we collect and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

  • Discord user IDs for economy/game tracking.
  • Server and channel IDs to send messages and track activity.

2. How Data is Used

Data is used strictly for:

  • Tracking user economy balances, game results, and stats.
  • Delivering bot messages and responses in Discord servers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Only the bot owner has access to necessary bot data.

4. Data Security

All data is stored securely and used solely for the bot’s functionality. In case of a breach, the owner will take necessary steps to mitigate risks.

5. Children’s Privacy

The bot is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Support

For questions about privacy, join our Discord Server.
